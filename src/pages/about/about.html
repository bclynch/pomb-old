About Pack On My Back
Pack On My Back is software platform intended to take the pain out of archiving your memories. No other service out there has created a satisfactory mix of tracking, blogging, sharing, and visualizing a journey so we decided to create it ourselves. Whether you want to describe every detail down to the last footstep or are okay keeping a loose journal of your travels, Pack On My Back is ready to make bringing your adventures to life fast and easy.
The Team (of one)
Pack On My Back is created and maintained by Brendan Lynch. Keep an eye out for him out on a trek looking like a dweeb in his zorries or cruising the streets on his bike.
At Pack On My Back we've set out to make the most comprehensive, intuitive mobile tracking tool in the world. Join the community of fellow travelers and outdoor enthusiasts to learn from its stories and inspire others along the way. We are a collective that will continue to grow and evolve along with you in your future endeavors. If you like our application spread the word and pack on.
